Once upon a time, Trisha Tobias sold her soul to the art world. Now, she is a senior Graphic Design student at Old Dominion University, taking on the world one coffee cup at a time.

While growing up, I wanted to be a lot of things (from an archaeologist, to an animator, to scientist, to an alien conspiracy theorist—just to name a few), but I eventually settled into the role of a designer, which, I suppose, lets me dabble in all those different areas in one way or another.

Right after high school, I left my hometown of Manila, Philippines, and flew over here to the United States to get a B.F.A. in Graphic Design. It has, since then, taken over my life.

Interests: making things, print, web, lettering, typography, writing, books, fantasy, paper crafts, music, piano, riddles, puzzles, math, science, cats, drawing, painting, charcoal, ink, artventures, making the world a better place, human rights, life, love, and the universe.
