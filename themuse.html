Website

These are the things kept in mind in this redesign: simplicity and ease-of-use while still delivering the necessary information. The original website is very content-heavy and hard-to-navigate, burying the two most important things to the organization: its educational workshops and writer events.

Speculative Work:

The Muse Writers Center

For the homepage, those two things are given focus. Users could quickly view the details for the class, and see what events are coming up. Clicking on the event location shows a map with the option to get directions to the area. Moreover, the homepage features a call-to-action for the organization’s Facebook page and newsletter.

Classes could be filtered by category, and the registration information is rearranged into several sections.
