Then Tell Them

The Coney Island Circus Sideshow features live acts of the bizarre and fantastical nature by professional performers. It happens every summer season, running from May through September, and is held at the Sideshow by the Seashore, from which the concept of this poster is based upon.

This is a project for my Poster class in Old Dominion University, and depicts Coney Island as this strange object that is of value.

I thought that it would be fun to get the style of the sideshow posters and the vintage beach postcards referencing to its rather vibrant history, and find a happy medium between those two. I painted this digitally, building up from a sketch and used a combination of the hard brush and the airbrush. The type is drawn and refined in Illustrator.
