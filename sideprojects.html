Then Tell Them — ongoing

www.thentellthem.tumblr.com

Graphic Design Studio project: What does the world need?

One of my weaknesses ever since was showing affection; I'm always on my guard, and in the process, there are a lot of things left unsaid for fear of saying too much or being awkward. There is always the assumption that "Oh, they probably get it." But how long will the people I care about be here? Do I matter to someone? Have I told someone that they matter to me?

This project is an exploration of relationships and the self, and partly a campaign to get people thinking about how others treat them and how they, in turn, treat other people, particularly those whom they are close to.
