Instacycle

Instacycle is a mash-up of the Instagram, Gumby and Cycle Trader APIs, and is an entry by Team Meh for Dominion Enterprises' Hackathon 6. It won the Best in Show category.

Team Meh: Odean Maye, Jose Mateo, Lookmai Rattana, Stanley Zheng, Benedict Cipcon, Trisha Tobias

The application functions as a search engine for motorcycles, with the feature of using user images from social media in addition to the stock photos shown in the Cycle Trader website. This allows motorcycle enthusiasts to see these cycles in action, and see if these are for sale/trade in Cycle Trader.

Since Instagram plays a big part in this application, I decided not to stray too far from the brand. For the logo, I combined imagery of a camera, and the front of the motorcycle. Since the team wanted a simple, clean app, but with a motorcycle-edgy feel, I kept it simple by showcasing the imagery. I played up on the gritty road feel through the grey, yellow and white color scheme and subtle textures.

Photographs are from Instagram and Cycle Trader. iPhone templates are from Graphic Burger.
