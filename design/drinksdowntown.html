Drinks Downtown

Vinylmint hosts a monthly gathering of creative professionals called Drinks Downtown.

During my internship in the Summer 2013 semester, I was tasked to redesign their logo, as well as create a mock-up of their event page to be developed and implemented by Lookmai Rattana.

The concept of the logo is a combination of a shot glass, conversations, the city, and the retro style. I decided to stick with a blue-green and orange color palette in honor of the city of Norfolk, Virginia.

Team: Byron Morgan, Lookmai Rattana, Trisha Tobias
