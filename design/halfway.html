Halfway App

Halfway is a mobile application, and is an entry for HackVirginia. It won second place in the hackathon.

Demo of mock-ups

Team Jose Mateo (Back-end), Chelsea Hunt, Le'Quinn Fields, Trisha Tobias (Front-end and design)

The application calculates the midpoint between two locations. Within that radius, points are highlighted, which shows the places that users could meet. This is based on the chosen meeting place the user inputted.

APIs used: Google Maps, Google Geocoding, Google Distance Matrix, Google Places, Twilio

iPhone templates are from Graphic Burger.
