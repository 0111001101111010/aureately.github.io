Room to Read

Room to Read is an organization that aims to provide education to children in developing countries by building schools and libraries, and in the process, communities.

This poster is a project for my Poster class in Old Dominion University, and shows how reading could take someone to places. Literacy bridges gaps, providing people more opportunities in terms of careers and in terms of making their communities a better place.

For this, I decided to try a more playful illustration style, with the header type drawn to mimic walking.
